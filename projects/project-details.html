Nova Studio

This project focused on crafting a digital presence that connects storytelling with design building a modern, fluid interface meant to engage users through every interaction. The goal was to translate Nova Studio’s creativity into a seamless online experience that feels both minimal and dynamic.

Client

Nova Studio

Role

UX/UI Designer, Visual Direction

Tools

Figma, Webflow, Illustrator

Visual journey

Behind the Design

The design direction aimed to blend minimalism with luxury, emphasizing texture and contrast to mirror the tactile essence of premium goods. The visual language is clean yet bold, ensuring that every product detail takes center stage.

  1. Discover: Define the brand’s essence and the feeling it should evoke.
  2. Explore: Experiment with compositions, grids, and lighting to achieve balance.
  3. Refine: Polish interactions and micro-details that elevate the digital experience.

The Result

The result is a refined digital experience that feels both exclusive and approachable. The site reflects Nova Studio’s dedication to quality through fluid motion, rich imagery, and a harmonious layout. Each scroll invites the user into a world of craftsmanship and modern design where elegance meets precision.

“Working with Eclipsis was a pleasure clear communication, thoughtful design, and a result beyond expectations.”

Sarah L., Founder at Nova Studio
